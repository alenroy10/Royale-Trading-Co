About us

Rooted in Kerala.
Trusted by global buyers.

Royale Trading Co is an Indian spice exporter supplying Green Cardamom, Black Pepper, Turmeric, Cloves, Cinnamon and other premium spices to buyers in Europe, UAE, USA and Southeast Asia. We are based in Idukki, Kerala—the heartland of India's finest cardamom production.

Our journey began with a simple goal: connecting international buyers with genuinely premium spices sourced directly from the farmers who cultivate them.

By working closely with growers in the Western Ghats, we ensure every batch maintains the qualities that make Kerala spices world-renowned—rich aroma, vibrant color, high essential oil content, and consistent grading.

From green cardamom to black pepper, cinnamon, cloves, and dry ginger, we deliver carefully selected products backed by transparent sourcing and reliable export support.

  • Direct sourcing from farmer networks across Idukki
  • Export-ready processing and packaging
  • Custom grading according to buyer requirements
  • Documentation support for international shipments
  • Certified Quality: APEDA, FSSAI, and Spice Board compliant
  • Long-term partnerships built on quality and trust
